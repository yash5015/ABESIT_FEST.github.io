International Conference on Futuristic Engineering Science & Technology

FEST 2023

February 24-25, 2023

ABES Institute of Technology Ghaziabad, India

*IC-FEST 2023 Publisher:
Proceedings Elsevier Procedia Computer Science (Scopus)
Special Issue in International Journal of Computer & Electrical Engineering, Elsevier (SCI)
Book Chapters in Edited book with CRC Press and Taylor & Francis (Scopus)

We are highly delighted to announce International Conference to be held at ABES Institute of Technology Ghaziabad, India, Uttar Pradesh, India on February 24-25, 2023 in blended mode. The conference is jointly organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering and Department of Information Technology. This conference will provide an International Forum for academicians, researchers, scientists, scholars, students and industry professionals to exchange information and share experiences in specialized interdisciplinary fields. The conference features traditional/modern style of paper presentations and keynote speaks by professionals. The speakers will focus on state-of-the-art engineering, scientific and technological evolutions that are being flourishing for transformation of us in to the new world. This forum can also be utilized by undergraduate, post graduate and PhD scholars to interact with top notch researchers on various innovational aspects and obtain the solution of their problems. About ABES Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad

ABES Institute of Technology, embarked on its mission in the field of Technical Education in the year 2007. It is situated on a prominent location at National Highway-09, that is approximately 19 km east of New Delhi. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The institute offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Computer Science & Engineering in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data Science (DS), Internet of Things (IoT) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE). B.Tech. in CSE & IT are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) New Delhi. Institute also offers Master of Computer Applications (MCA) degree course.

The Institute has bagged various awards in academic, innovation and sports activities. Teaching learning atmosphere in the campus has shown its impact on overall performance of the students in Smart India Hackathon (SIH) consistently with consolation prize in 2017, and winners under one problem statement in 2018, 2019 & 2020. Institutions Innovation Council (IIC) is consistently securing 4.0 STAR ratings till this academic year 2021-22. The Institute is also recognized as “Band-Performer” under the category “Colleges/Institutes (Private/Self-Financed) (Technical)” in ARIIA ranking 2021. ABESIT’s Centre of Excellences covers different field of engineering like IoT and Analytics (Entuple & CDAC); Analog and Embedded Systems (Texas Instruments); e-Yantra in Embedded Systems & Robotics; Data Science, AI-ML (Edugrad); Industrial Hydraulics (Rexroth Bosch, Germany); Industrial-Pneumatics (FESTO, Germany) exposing students with latest corporate developments and requirements to make them industry job fit.

Recently, the Institute has set up of an AI based Centre of Excellence in association with NVIDIA for students and faculty where they will be provided exposure to industry standards with high-quality in a competitive and challenging environment. It is the first-of-its-kind Centre of Excellence established in any private institute in affiliating university of Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, and NCR Region. NVIDIA’s AI computing stack is the most advanced in the world. From data processing to AI model training to model deployment & inferencing, their solution addresses end-to-end development of AI.

About Department of Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, established in the year 2007 and is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) New Delhi till June 2025. The department has well equipped labs as well as qualified & experienced teaching faculty. Apart from teaching the university prescribed curriculum, department regularly conducts workshops, seminars, industrial visits and guest lectures by the experts from industry and academics. Students get proper platform to showcase their capabilities and they are made to participate in group discussions, personal interviews. Students are deriving benefits from following Center of Excellences by participating in various training programs that includes Advanced Robotic Control for Industrial Automation, Big Data, Block Chain and IOT.

About Department of Information Technology

The Department offers Bachelor of Technology program in Information Technology is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) New Delhi till June 2025. The faculty and staff members of Department are well qualified and efficient in guiding the students in every field of the Information Technology. The area of Information Technology aims at design, implementation and management of computer-based information systems. These automated information systems are capable of storing, transmitting and retrieving of all types of complex data. The Department develops IT Graduates having good problem-solving capabilities, communication skills and habit to learn new technologies. Apart from prescribed university curriculum, focus is on practical and skill-based training to ensure that students grow according to changing market scenario.
